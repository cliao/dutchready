Effective

Our lessons are one-on-one private sessions and focus on conversational fluency and learning through understanding so that you get the most bang for your buck. Our friendly tutors are language enthusiasts with a passion for education, motivated to get you speaking Dutch in no time.

Flexible

No more cycling through the rain, no more rushing to a lesson. Our lessons take place in the comfort of your own home, at a time that suits you. We flexibly plan our hours so that we can work around your schedule with ease.

Customizable

Our lessons are set at your pace and your level. We focus on topics that you want to learn, for situations you want to feel confident in. This way learning is suitable, useful and above all fun.
