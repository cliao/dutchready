Quality Private Tutors

We take great care in finding you the right tutor. Our friendly tutors are language enthusiasts, motivated to get you speaking Dutch in no time. Through our personal assessment, we match you with a tutor that's right for your needs – be it speaking, spelling or grammar.

Flexible Scheduling

No more cycling through the rain, no more rushing to a lesson. Our lessons take place where you want them to. Take Dutch lessons at home for extra comfort, or spice it up and visit your favorite café. We plan our hours weekly so that we can work around your schedule flexibly.

Customizable Lesson Plans

Don't waste time learning things you don't need. Private lessons are set at your pace and your level. We focus on topics that you want to learn, for situations you want to feel confident in. Be it striking up a conversation with co-workers, meeting new people, or just ordering a bite to eat. This way you learn Dutch for situations you need, even as that need changes.

Dutch Ready has a great approach to teaching. As opposed to simply repeating words or phrases (as is often the case), Dutch Ready focuses on teaching through understanding, and makes understanding difficult concepts seem relatively simple!

Hoi! Ik ben Casper.

As an eager learner and self proclaimed geek I've always had a passion for education. It started when I moved from The Netherlands to the U.S. as a child, without speaking a single word of English. All I could do was try to learn. I know how powerful it can be to break down the language barrier and be able to speak to someone openly. Through Dutch Ready I want to share that feeling with others.

I want all our students to be as passionate about our lessons as we are. And I'm confident you will be, right after the first lesson! That's why we always do a personal assessment before starting so that your level, learning style and personality match well with that of your tutor. But if you're not satisfied, we make it super easy to get your money back.
